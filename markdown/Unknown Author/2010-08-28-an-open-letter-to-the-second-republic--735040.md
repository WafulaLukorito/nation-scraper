## What you need to know:

* The vultures are circling ahead. They will either paralyse and kill you, or you will exterminate them

This open letter to “Second Republic” is an exhortation. You cannot hear me yet because you are but an idea, even though you are three days old.

Nor can you see me because your eyes are still undeveloped. You lie in your crib as a concept on a piece of paper.

You are a state in embryo, vulnerable and helpless. You cannot fend for yourself. You will perish if left alone without nurture.

There are dark and foreboding forces circling overhead. Their public talk may be sweet but their tongues are forked. The most evil will try to malnourish, mutilate, or even murder you.

Chosen One, why do you think they would invite President Omar al-Bashir, a man indicted for genocide by the International Criminal Court (ICC), to your birthday? You are in mortal danger.

Remember the fate of your older brother, “First Republic.” By his fifth birthday, he was unrecognisable because they had mutilated him beyond recognition.

He was a tortured soul, crippled by his parents at birth. He lived a life of misery, and never realised his enormous potential. He was killed so that you could be born. He died on August 27, 2010 at Uhuru Park, and you were born the moment he breathed his last.

There was an unprecedented orgy of celebration by the people. Not since 1964 – when your brother was born – had there been such a party. But I write this open letter to warn you. Don’t believe the revellers.

Enjoy the applause, but don’t believe it. The plebeians mean well. But it is not them I am worried about. I am most concerned about your “legal” parents and their relatives. They have treacherous hearts. Many have been known to “eat their young.”

Wanjiku is not your problem. She – in the guise of civil society, the Ufungamano Initiative, and so-called “Second Liberators” – fought so that you could be born.

But Wanjiku did not give “legal” birth to you. You are her idea, but not her “legal” child. You have her genetic fingerprint, but you don’t belong to her.

I know Wanjiku is your “biological mother,” but your “legal father” is the political class. Your “legal parents” and their relatives are the problem.

You saw what happened to your brother – “First Republic” – in 2008 after the 2007 elections. His “legal” parents and relatives cut him up with machetes.

Only the kindness of strangers – led by a Ghanaian called Kofi Annan – saved him from certain death. But he was so badly maimed that his death was guaranteed. He was laid to rest last Friday.

What worries me most is that you have the same “legal” parents as “First Republic.” For now, your “legal” parents are saying all the right things.

This, rather paradoxically, includes those who wanted to “abort” you! That’s why you must be afraid – very afraid. How can they do a 180-degree turn in a matter of weeks? You must smell a big fat rat if you are like me!

This is why I believe the vultures are circling overhead. You were born to put an end to their suzerainty over Kenya. You, “Second Republic,” are designed to make the tormentors of Kenya extinct. You were born to be a saviour.

But the vultures want to co-opt and corrupt you so that the plunder can continue. That’s why they will not let you end impunity.

Your birth is a mortal danger to them. One of two things will happen. Either they will paralyse and eventually kill you, or you will exterminate them.

There is no compromise, or middle path. It is all, or nothing. So the toothy smiles you see today are the fake facades of schemers. They will lie in wait for a chance to pounce.

Your success, “Second Republic,” will mean the end of the vultures. Ironically, these are your “legal” parents.

But make no mistake. There is nothing sentimental about the coming contest. Forget about all that nonsense about blood being thicker than water.

You have come to dispossess them of ill-gotten gain. In their place, you want the new Kenyan. That’s why your interests are diametrically opposed to theirs.

They may be smiling today, but they will be seeing red tomorrow when you commence real reforms. If you are to succeed, there must be pain. As they say, there is no gain without pain.

Beware that the vultures have a way of “outliving” and “outmanoeuvring” reform. They have done it since the birth of “First Republic.”

I know that some of the vultures will tell you that I have a jaundiced eye. Don’t believe them. Didn’t you see what happened at Uhuru Park? There were a lot of vultures on the dais, and not many true reformers.

The same vultures who destroyed “First Republic” claimed you as their baby. They held you in their clawed hands. Some gave you what they hope is a kiss of death. It happened to “First Republic” when the Mau Mau were swept aside by the home guards.

You must be very careful because history knows how to repeat itself. I hear already that the vultures are running for the Senate and the county governorships. They want to surround you and close off all exits.

My crystal ball tells me that you will only succeed if you run – very fast – to Wanjiku. She is your “biological” mother and will protect and nurture you. If you don’t embrace her, you will be short-lived.

Don’t condemn yourself to a life of Sisyphus. You were born to transform Kenya. Go get ‘em!

*Makau Mutua is Dean and SUNY Distinguished Professor at the State University of New York at Buffalo Law School and Chair of the KHRC.*
