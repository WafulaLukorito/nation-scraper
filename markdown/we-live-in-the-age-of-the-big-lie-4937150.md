We live in an age of the Big Lie. The bigger the lie the better. It’s a universe where facts don’t seem to matter. In an age where some in society want us to believe that what we see isn’t real or factual.

Even those who are supposedly responsible would have us live in a fact-free world. At no time in recent history have established norms come under such severe challenge. Revisionism is the order of the day. What was upright yesterday is upside down today. Yesteryear’s lie is today’s truth. America’s Donald Trump, the proto-fascist, is now the democrat. Beautiful is now ugly. There is no reality, really, except opinions. How, when, and why did we get here?

For one, history does indeed repeat itself. Not in exactly the same way, but it does come full circle. Look at South Africa, for example. Time was when South Africa was an apartheid state. In case you forgot, that isn’t ancient history, but in the 1990s. Like South Africa, the United States was also still an apartheid state in the 1960s.

Two nations in one – one Black, the other White. A thing as simple as skin colour determined your fate and destiny in both countries. A big lie about skin colour as a referent about your ability, intelligence, and station in life. Then came the civil rights and anti-apartheid movements. We thought racism was over. No – it hid for only a few years.

## Racism

Racism of White over Black, even of colourism, is virulently back. As a big lie: that White Supremacy can “Make America Great Again;” that Palestinians are a worthless people and can be wiped off the face of the earth by Israel; that the United States can ethnically cleanse Gaza and gift it to Jews.

The big lie, which has come back with a fury, is that Palestinians are sub-human; that White Americans in the Age of Trump have a manifest destiny to rule over Black, Brown, and Asian peoples as of right anywhere on earth. This is a big lie that even some Black, Brown, and Asian people echo without shame, or irony. They bequeath their White tormentors the cudgel of repression.

Second, illusionists and propagandists roam the planet. Because of technology and inventions like Artificial Intelligence, every evil and criminal mind is a click away from deploying the big lie. What was supposed to democratise how information and knowledge are garnered and deployed has now become a weapon of mass destruction as the big lie.

Anyone with a device anywhere can use it to torment another without virtually any consequences. At least not yet. Jealous, small-minded minions scour social media to inflict mental anguish on those whom they loathe or fear. Your success, or good fortune, is their grist of the mill to spew their smallness. The big lie is that social media was going to level the information and knowledge playing field. Instead, it’s become a sewer.

## Backlash

Third, the big lie is part of the backlash. Of course, history doesn’t move in a straight line — or even progressively forward. Sometimes, it can take steps backwards. Again, on race. It’s now fashionable in the United States – which many White people mistakenly believe belongs to them by some divine right – to act and brag openly with pride about White Supremacy.

What’s ironic is that more Black, Brown, and Asian peoples than ever before are risking everything to go to America, many dying along the way on the US-Mexico border. The big lie is that America is a land flowing with milk and honey. America is an exceptional God-chosen country, we are told. The city on a shining hill.

Fourth, once we used to revile colonialism and White bigotry. In fact, the anti-colonial struggle for the peoples of the Global South against those of the Global North was an article of faith for human liberation. Not a sane person could gainsay it. It’s a logic that was long used to germinate theories of underdevelopment in the Global South.

You will recall seminal works like Walter Rodney’s *How Europe Underdeveloped Africa.* That book is now treated by big liars as a crybaby’s excuse for the crises in Africa. The revisionists would have you believe that Africa is solely, or mostly, responsible for its own underdevelopment. Stop crying about colonialism, or the cold war. Man up, or shut up!

Lastly, I think that the election of Barack Obama as the first Black president of the United States was the reason for the emergence of Mr Trump. The big lie that a Black man and his Black spouse could dare sleep in Lincoln’s bedroom – in the White (white) House – is the comeback.

That the “white house” had to be cleansed by Mr Trump. It was the impetus for the January 6 Insurrection, or coup attempt, by White Supremacists in 2021 against Joe Biden’s election victory. The Big Lie is alive and well. Can we defeat it?
